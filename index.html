About

Graduated Bachelor of Science in Business Administration with a Major in Finance in ’97. Speak Turkish and English fluently. Has attained extensive work experience for over 25 years in private sector. Experienced in Budgeting, Financial Reporting, Cash-Flow Analysis, Business Planning and Development, Project Management, Quality Management Systems, Software Conversions, Stringent Financial Workflows, Risk Management and Risk & Control Management Processes. Up until recently involved in projects for Fleet Management, Vehicular Technologies and Telematics Security & Safety Solutions.

Currently, I am pivoting my extensive background into Data Science, specializing in Python and SQL. My objective is to synthesize a quarter-century of financial expertise with modern data analytics to drive deeper insights, automate complex workflows, and create high-impact socio-economic content. I am also pursuing my passion for digital content creation and visual arts. I specialize in still-life and moving images, producing socio-economic content that synthesizes analytical precision with high-impact visual storytelling.
